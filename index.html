FAQ

What is time.shop?

time.shop is a decentralized time marketplace where anyone can buy, sell, and book time using a unified currency, $TIME, enabling a fully liquid and tradable time economy.

How is time.shop different from traditional booking platforms?

Unlike traditional platforms that lock users into rigid bookings, time.shop allows free trading, reselling, and staking of time, creating a flexible and dynamic time economy.

How does pricing work on time.shop?

time.shop uses a bonding curve pricing model, meaning the cost of booking time fluctuates based on real-time demand and availability.

Who can sell their time on time.shop?

Anyone. Whether you're a freelancer, consultant, creator, or coach, you can list your time, define its purpose, and set your own rate. No application, approval, or intermediary is needed.

Can I resell time that I've already purchased?

Yes. All time assets are tokenized, meaning you can resell, transfer, or gift your booked time slots to others‚Äîbefore the session is redeemed. This gives full liquidity to time.

What happens if a seller misses or cancels a session?

If the seller fails to fulfill the booking, the buyer is automatically refunded via smart contract. No disputes, no support tickets‚Äîjust trustless protection.

How can I earn rewards on time.shop?

Users can stake $TIME, participate in liquidity pools, or earn rewards through time farming by engaging in marketplace activities.

Is time.shop fully decentralized?

Yes, time.shop operates on a trustless, smart contract-driven system, with no intermediaries or centralized control over transactions.

How does governance work?

$TIME holders can participate in time.shop DAO, voting on fee structures, platform upgrades, and ecosystem developments.

What blockchain is time.shop built on?

time.shop is deployed on Solana for high-speed, low-cost transactions, with future plans for multi-chain expansion.

Can businesses use time.shop?

Yes, companies can tokenize consulting hours, employee schedules, and expert services, allowing corporate clients to book and trade time seamlessly.
